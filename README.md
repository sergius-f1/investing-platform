# Investment Platform

A lightweight AI-native investment platform for portfolio analysts.

---

## Feature: Portfolio Signal Dashboard

Investment analysts need a fast way to assess portfolio state and act on it.

The dashboard gives an analyst a single view of their portfolio — what they hold, how it's performing, and what the system recommends doing with each position.

For each holding, the platform generates a signal: **BUY**, **HOLD**, or **REDUCE** — based on momentum and volatility of the asset. Each signal comes with a confidence score and a short rationale, so the analyst understands why the recommendation was made.

At the top of the dashboard, the analyst sees a portfolio-level summary: total value, daily PnL, risk score, and number of open positions. An AI insight panel provides a human-readable interpretation of the overall portfolio state based on the current signals.

The goal is to reduce the time an analyst spends assembling a picture of the portfolio — and surface actionable signals faster.
